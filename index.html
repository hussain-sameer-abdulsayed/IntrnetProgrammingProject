How to Use the App

Follow these simple steps to get started:

  1. Add a Task: Click on "Add To-Do" to create a new task by entering a title and description.
  2. View Tasks: View all your tasks on the "View To-Dos" page.
  3. Complete a Task: Mark tasks as complete by clicking the "Mark as Complete" button.
  4. Delete a Task: Delete completed tasks when they are no longer needed.
