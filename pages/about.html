About the To-Do App

The To-Do App is a simple, user-friendly application designed to help you stay organized and manage your tasks efficiently. Whether you're a student, a professional, or just someone trying to keep track of daily activities, this app provides the tools you need to get things done.

Key Features

  • Add tasks with a title and description.
  • View all tasks in a single list.
  • Mark tasks as complete to track your progress.
  • Delete tasks once they're no longer needed.

Why Use This App?

Staying organized has never been easier. With the To-Do App, you can prioritize your tasks, avoid missing deadlines, and focus on what's most important. The clean and intuitive design makes it accessible for users of all experience levels.
